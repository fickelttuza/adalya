WHO WE ARE

Adalya Tobacco is a globally recognized brand founded by a tobacco expert born in 1963, who got his degree in Tobacco Technology in 1986. From the start, Adalya aimed to be a global leader and achieved worldwide recognition. We built our own cutting-edge factory in 2016, now one of the largest in the industry, and have produced top-quality hookah tobacco there since 2017. In 2023, with the aim to become the world leader, Adalya expanded into the Middle East and North American markets. Our products meet EU standards and are made from the highest quality tobacco. Adalya is the largest hookah tobacco producer in Turkey and the second largest globally, with a growing global market share.
